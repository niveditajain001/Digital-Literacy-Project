Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
I used Canva to create my Digital Literacy Awareness poster. Canva’s templates and design features helped me make a clean and visually appealing layout. I then customized the design by editing text, adding icons, and adjusting sections to improve clarity. My infographic explains the concept of digital literacy and includes safe internet practices and useful digital tools such as Google Docs, Canva, Zoom, and GitHub. I also added a section explaining why digital literacy matters. One challenge I faced was balancing text and visuals without overcrowding the design. This task helped me understand the importance of visual communication.
